Our mission

0xVentures is a decentralized autonomous organization (DAO) that functions as a treasury with the sole purpose of investing in sector-disrupting projects. These projects are researched in both an external macro setting and micro discourse with the teams. Our recognition both as a brand and individuals, provides us a unique opportunity to drive engagement across many blockchain communities.

Our value

We pride ourselves in being value-add investors. With diverse backgrounds and expertise, we provide a unique perspective to the projects in terms of marketing, UI/UX design, financing and programming. This also opens up our investment strategy to multiple sectors, including blockchain specific protocols, gaming, NFTs, decentralised exchanges and many more.

Why DAO?

We are an internet-native organisation which is collectively owned and managed by our members. We have no central leadership, anyone can introduce a change or proposal, it is then discussed and voted on as a community. 0xVentures believes that the DAO format is the ideal organizational path to foster community, creativity and more informed decision making.

Our members

Each member is critically chosen. This is done to foster an environment where we work together as one unit that shares, evolves and brings ideas into fruition.
